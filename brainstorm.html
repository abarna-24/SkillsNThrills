PHYSICAL ARTS (ABS)

CHALLENGE YOUR SKILL
Rules:
  • The participants will be expected to telecast their yoga mastery in a series of challenging poses , sequences and breathing exercises.
  • The duration of the video content must be between one and five minutes.
  • Making the video in a portrait manner is convenient to post on social media.
  • First, explain your challenge in the video, and then go for it.
FITNESS KNOWLEDGE
Rules:
  • The individual participant will have to showcase their expertise in fitness knowledge through speaking or practical demonstrations.
  • The duration of the video content must be between one and one and a half minutes.
  • Making the video in a portrait manner is convenient to post on social media.
  • The content should be clear.
GYMNASTICS
Rules:
  • The participants are expected to showcase their gymnastics like flips , dance and jumps with cool moves and flexibility.
  • It may be a picture or video.
  • The duration of the video content must be within one minute.
  • Making the video in a portrait manner is convenient to post on social media.
KARATE AND BOXING
Rules:
  • The participants are expected to make and explain a situation in which the person is in danger.
  • Make a video to protect themselves or to raise awareness about how to escape this situation.
  • The duration of the video content must be between one and one and a half minutes.
  • Making the video in a portrait manner is convenient to post on social media.



CALISTHENICS
Rules:
  • The participants will be expected to do a maximum number of push-ups or pull-ups within 1 minute.
  • The duration of the video content must be within one minute.
  • Making the video in a portrait manner is convenient to post on social media.
General Instructions
Rules:
  • Registration for Abs will be open up to March 10.
  • The video should not exceed 5 minutes for the events Fitness Knowledge , Challenge your skill (Yoga),Gymnastics ,Karate andBoxing (Fighting skill).
  • Make sure that the audio and video are properly synced.
  • The medium of language must be none other than Tamil or English.
  • The video must be clear ,including the face,and maintain a slight distance from the camera.
  • Making the video in a portrait manner is convenient to post on social media.
  • Make sure that participants are joined in their respective WhatsApp groups after filling out the form if mentioned.
